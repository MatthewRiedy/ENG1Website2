The Heslington Hustle: A Student’s Journey

Welcome to “The Heslington Hustle”, a unique single-player game that simulates the life of a second-year computer science student during the crucial final week before exams. This website serves as a comprehensive resource for understanding the game's details. Here, you can also access our project’s source code and download the game. Navigate through the hustle of academic life, make strategic decisions, and strive for success in the world of academia. Explore our website to learn more about the game and embark on your own Heslington Hustle!
